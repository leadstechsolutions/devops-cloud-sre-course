The landscape

Five roles, one delivery system

[ DEV ]

DevOps Engineer

Automates build, test, release and rollback. Owns the delivery pipeline.

[ CLD ]

Cloud Engineer

Designs and builds the secure, scalable infrastructure apps run on.

[ SRE ]

Site Reliability Eng.

Keeps production healthy, observable and recoverable.

[ PLT ]

Platform Engineer

Builds reusable golden paths so other teams move fast safely.

[ OPS ]

Production Support

Keeps live systems running; triages, follows runbooks, escalates.

These aren't isolated job titles — they're connected disciplines that together move software to production reliably.

The reliability role

Site Reliability Engineer

  • Monitoring, alerting & dashboards
  • Incident response & on-call
  • SLIs, SLOs & error budgets
  • Runbooks & blameless postmortems
  • Automation to reduce toil; performance & capacity
SRE starts where deployment ends. Once something is live, we need to know whether it's healthy — and how to respond when it isn't.

A service slows at peak — the SRE asks

  • Down or just degraded? How many users?
  • Which metric changed — and did we deploy recently?
  • Errors in the logs? Is the database saturated?
  • Are we still meeting the SLO?
  • Fix now vs. improve later?

The adjacent roles

Platform Engineering & Production Support

[ PLT ] Platform Engineering

Reusable internal platforms and standards, so the right way is the easy way.

  • Standard CI/CD templates & Terraform modules
  • Kubernetes deploy templates & golden paths
  • Self-service portals & secure baselines
[ OPS ] Production Support

Keeps existing systems running and helps resolve live issues.

  • Ticket triage & application support
  • Log review, restarts & escalation
  • Known-issue docs & user-impact comms
Platform Engineering makes the right way the easy way. Many careers start in support or junior infra — valid stepping stones into all three tracks.

The bar

What "senior" actually means

RoleJunior signalSenior signal — what hiring managers look for
DevOps / PlatformFollows a template, fixes a buildDesigns org-wide CI/CD & golden paths; sets release policy; cuts lead time across teams
CloudCreates resources from a runbookOwns multi-account architecture, landing zones, network/IAM & cost guardrails; reviews others' IaC
SREAcks alerts, follows a runbookDefines SLOs & error budgets; leads incident command; drives postmortems; kills classes of toil

Junior engineers complete tickets. Senior engineers remove the reason the ticket existed.
